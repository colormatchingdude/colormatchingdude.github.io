How to Play

1

Observe the Target color on the right that you need to recreate.

2

Click on the colored circles below to add amounts of each base color to your mix.

3

Use the minus buttons to remove colors if you add too much of any color.

4

Try to get your match percentage as close to 100% as possible!

5

Click Reset to start over, Solution to see the answer, or Next for a new challenge.
